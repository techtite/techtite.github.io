WHO WE ARE

Techtite E-Sports Lounge is a computer cafe catered for gamers, competitive or casual. Our available games include trending games, single-player games and competitive games. We have two sections inside the cafe, the VIP section, and the standard section. The standard section has mid-high end computers with mechanical keyboards, gaming mice, and gaming headset, which can satisfy most customers. If you want to go all the way, however, the VIP section is for you. The VIP section is equipped with high-end gaming equipment that can run games smoothly with maximum quality and enables you to keep that killstreak going while maintaining maximum comfort.
